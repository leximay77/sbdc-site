Recurring Events

Check out these recurring events in the Seattle area! We're lucky to have amazing instructors, musicians, DJs, venues, and dancers here in the Seattle area who meet up to dance, learn, and share their love of Blues.


  • Blues Underground ("BUG") @ Dance Underground in Capitol Hill

    Blues Underground has been Seattle's mainstay social Blues dance night since 2005. Blues Underground brings in world renowned instructors for workshops, host live music nights with local bands, and partners with local organizations supporting Seattle's Black community.


    Monthly on the third Friday

    • All-levels drop-in lesson 8:30-9:30pm
    • DJ'd music from 9:30pm-12am

  • Biweekly Socials @ Reverie Ballroom in Capitol Hill

    Come to the reopened and beautifully renovated Reverie Ballroom for Wednesday night Blues socials twice a month. These take place in the Emerald ballroom and feature a short drop-in lesson at the start. Also look out for the recurring series classes before these socials, and then practice what you learn on the social floor!


    Second and Fourth Wednesdays of each Month

    • All-levels drop-in lesson 9-9:30pm
    • DJ'd music from 9:30pm-12am

    • www.reverieballroom.com

  • Hillman City Sway @ Black & Tan Hall in Rainier Valley

    Join us once a month for Hillman City Sway! Presented in partnership with Black & Tan Hall and Blues Underground. Featuring a swanky evening of local live music, DJ's and dance instructors.


    Monthly on the fourth Thursday

    • Mixer and all-levels drop-in lesson 8-9pm
    • Band plays 9-11pm
    • All ages welcome!

    • blackandtanhall.com

  • What The Blues @ Lowdown Ballroom in Capitol Hill


    Lesson Series & Drop-Ins

    Our core weekly lesson timeslot is Tuesdays 6:30-8:30pm, at the Lowdown Ballroom. We will either have drop-in lessons, or 4- or 5-weeks series, depending on the month. Cancellations or special events occur occasionally, so make sure to check the calendar!


    Workshops

    Occasionally, we will teach workshops on more specific topics or styles. We also bring in out-of-town and national/international instructors to share their knowledge and experience with Blues.


    Private Lessons

    If you're after a more tailored learning experience, we also offer private lessons. Our current rates are $90 - $125 / hr, sliding scale. Please contact us to set up a time!
